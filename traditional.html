Traditional Textiles of The World

Batik (Malaysia)

Batik Malaysia is a traditional textile art form knownfro its vibrant colors and intricate designs, often inspired by nature, such as flowers and leaves.

Kente (Ghana)

Colorful, handwoven cloth traditionally worn by royalty. Each pattern and color has symbolic meaning.

Silk Sari (India)

A traditional draped garment made from luxurious silk. Known for intricate embroidery and bright colors.

Batik (Indonesia)

A wax-resist dyeing technique where intricate patterns are drawn with wax before dyeing. Often used for ceremonial wear.
